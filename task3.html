More About The Sun

The Sun is an ordinary star, one of about 100 billion in our galaxy,the Milky Way.It is a huge ball of hydrogen and helium held together by its own gravity.It drives weather, ocean currents, seasons and climate, and makes plant life possible through photosynthesis.

More About The Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known where life developed and found habitability.This is enabled by Earth being a water world ,the only one in the Solar system sustaining liquid surface water.

More About The Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite. Its diameter is about one-quarter of Earth's, making it the fifth largest satellite in the Solar System and the largest and most massive relative to its parent planet. It is larger than all known dwarf planets in the Solar System.
